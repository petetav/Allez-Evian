Perched on the southern shore of Lac Leman is an historic apartment block with dramatic views over the lake. Apartment 3 is a ground floor apartment offering easy access to the garden and all the other amenities.


Key features:

  • Spacious ground floor apartment on the lakeshore
  • Stunning views of sunrise and sunset
  • Walking distance to Evian centre
  • Easy access to Geneva, Verbier, Zermat, Lausanne and Les Portes de Soleil
  • Private/communal swimming pool, parking, gardens and marina

Outside


There are electric gates leading to two private parking places (plus more for visitors) and then a choice of entrances to the apartment; one via the lawn and one via the communal entrance hall. Within the gated area there is a garden, a marina, a swimming pool and even a small private beach.

The sun terrace, along the shore of the lake is a fantastic place to enjoy the tranquility of the waves lapping against the shoreline and offers direct access to the lake. The door leading from the terrace goes down some steps onto the lawn which leads onto the bridge, marina, swimming pool and garden area. Hopefully the photographs do a better job of describing the place than I do!

Location


Le Lumina is 40 minutes from the ski resorts of Verbier, Zermat, and the Portes de Soleil (Morzine, Avoriaz etc). By car it's 45 minutes to Geneva and less to Montreux. It's a 15 minute walk to Evian via a newly constructed pretty lakeside park and chic restaurants. The ferry from there takes 30 minutes to reach Lausanne. Lausanne railway station is big and very well connected. There is a train every 15 minutes or so direct to Geneva and its airport.


Inside


This is a very spacious 4 bedroom apartment with a fantastic view over Lac Leman. The total interior space is 210 square metres and comprises 4 bedrooms, a living room, an open plan kitchen and a large sun terrace. The floorplan below is taken from the surveyors drawing and is to scale.

In the basement there are two utility rooms, one equipped for a washing machine and tumble-dryer. There is also a communal room equipped for bicycle storage.
